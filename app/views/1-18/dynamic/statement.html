Requesting a statement of achievement

Learners who withdraw from their T Level course will not receive a T Level certificate. Instead, you must request a statement of achievement, which shows the T Level components they completed.

Before you request a statement of achievement

! Warning As a provider, you must first tell the exam board (also known as the awarding organisation) that the learner has withdrawn from their course before submitting a statement of achievement request.

After the exam board has updated the learner's registration status on this service, you can use this service to request a statement of achievement. You will need to confirm:

  • the learner's detail are correct
  • the learner's achievements are correct.

If the learner's details or achievements are wrong or missing

You can add or update a learner's English and maths achievement and industry placement completion status in the 'Manage learner T Level records' part of this service.

You need to speak to the exam board if the learner's details or technical qualification component results need changing.
