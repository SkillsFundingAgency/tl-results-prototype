Requesting a statement of achievement

Learners who withdraw from their T Level course will not receive a T Level certificate. Instead, you can request a statement of achievement, which shows the T Level components they completed.

Before you request a statement of achievement

As a provider, you need to confirm:

  • the learner has withdrawn from their T Level course
  • the learner's English and maths achievements are correct
  • the learner's industry placement completion status is correct.

Exam boards (also known as awarding organisations) are responsible for recording:

  • learner details
  • technical qualification component results.
