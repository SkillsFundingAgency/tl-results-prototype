What are the minimum standards?

The minimum standards are full level 2 qualifications (GCSE or equivalent).

You can adjust the minimum standards for English and maths for learners with special educational needs and/or a disability (SEND) on an individual case-by-case basis, where all of the following conditions have been met:

  • The learner has an existing or previously issued Education, Health and Care (EHC) Plan, a statement of Special Educational Need (SEN) or a Learning Difficulty Assessment (LDA)
  • The provider holds or has conducted an evidenced assessment demonstrating that even with support, reasonable adjustments and stepping stone qualifications the learner is not able to achieve English or maths to the minimum level as a result of their learning difficulty or disability
  • The learner is deemed able to successfully achieve all other aspects of the T Level requirements and achieve Entry Level 3 Functional Skills in the adjusted subject(s) before the end of their T Level
  • There are no industry specific minimum entry requirements

You do not need to upload any evidence, but you should hold supporting documentation in your own files.
