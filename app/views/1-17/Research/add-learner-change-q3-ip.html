What are special considerations?

Select 'Yes, completed with special consideration' only if you have made adjustments for learners who were unable to perform to the best of their ability during their placement due to exceptional circumstances. For example:

  • personal or family problems
  • physical or mental illness or injury
  • bereavement
  • safeguarding issues
  • withdrawal of initial placement offer

You do not need to tell us the reason or upload any evidence, but you should hold supporting documentation in your own files.
