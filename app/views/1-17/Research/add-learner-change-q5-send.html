What are the minimum standards?

The minimum standards are full level 2 qualifications (GCSE or equivalent).

If the learner has qualifications from outside England and Wales

You will need to check the learner's grades are equal to a full level 2 qualification and have met the minimum standard.

If the learner has special educational needs and/or a disability (SEND)

You can adjust the minimum standards for English and maths for learners with SEND if all the following conditions are met:

  • The learner has an Education, Health and Care (EHC) Plan, a statement of Special Educational Need (SEN) or a Learning Difficulty Assessment (LDA)
  • Your organisation holds an evidenced assessment showing that even with support, reasonable adjustments and stepping stone qualifications, the learner is not able to achieve English or maths to the minimum level as a result of their learning difficulty or disability
  • The learner is able to achieve all other aspects of the T Level and achieve Entry Level 3 Functional Skills in the adjusted subject(s) before the end of their T Level

You do not need to upload any evidence, but you should hold supporting documentation in your own records.
