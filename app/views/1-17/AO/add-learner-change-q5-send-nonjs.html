What's LRS?

The Learning Records Service (LRS) collects data relating to learners' post-14 qualifications, for example, GCSEs.

What are the minimum standards?

The minimum standards are full level 2 qualifications (GCSE or equivalent).

If the learner has qualifications from outside England and Wales

You will need to check the learner's grades are equal to a full level 2 qualification and have met the minimum standard.

If the learner has special educational needs and/or a disability (SEND)

You can adjust the minimum standards for English and maths for learners with SEND if the following conditions are met:

  • The learner has an Education, Health and Care (EHC) Plan, a statement of Special Educational Need (SEN) or a Learning Difficulty Assessment (LDA)
  • Your assessment of the learner shows that even with support, reasonable adjustments or stepping stone qualifications, the learner is not able to achieve a full level 2 qualification because of their learning difficulty or disability

If the learner has SEND and is studying an Entry Level 3 English and Maths qualification at the same time as their T Level

If the learner has not yet achieved their grade, please select 'Not achieved the minimum standard' and update this record when they have their grade.

You do not need to upload any evidence, but you should hold supporting documentation in your own records.

  • the learner has been assessed by our organisation and meets all the conditions required to adjust the minimum standards
  • the learner has met the minimum standard for English and maths as a SEND learner
