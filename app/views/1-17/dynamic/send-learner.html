More about SEND

SEND learners are supported by one of these documents:

  • Education, Health and Care (EHC) Plan
  • Statement of Special Educational Need (SEN)
  • Learning Difficulty Assessment (LDA)

Both of the following conditions must also be met:

  • The provider holds an evidenced assessment demonstrating that even with support, reasonable adjustments and stepping stone qualifications the learner is not able to achieve English or maths to the minimum level as a result of their learning difficulty or disability
  • The learner is deemed able to achieve all other aspects of the T Level and achieve Entry Level 3 Functional Skills in the adjusted subject(s) before the end of their T Level
