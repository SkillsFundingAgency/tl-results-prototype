Request statement of achievement

You must use this service to request a statement of achievement for a learner who has withdrawn from their T Level course.

The statement of achievement will show which T Level components the learner completed. They will not receive a T Level certificate.

Continue

Before you start

  1. Tell the exam board (also known as the awarding organisation) that the learner has withdrawn from their T Level course.
  2. Use the 'Manage learner T Level records' part of this service to confirm the learner's:
    • English and maths achievements; and
    • industry placement completion status.
