Cookie policy

Cookies are files saved on your phone, tablet or computer when you visit a website.

We only use cookies that are essential for you to use this service.

These essential cookies do things like:

  • remember which notifications you have seen so you are not shown them more than once
  • temporarily store the selections you make
  • keep you logged in during your browsing session

They are automatically enabled.
