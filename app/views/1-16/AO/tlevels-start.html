Sign in to manage your T Level results

As an awarding organisation you can use this service to:

  • review your T Level details
  • tell us which providers are offering your T Levels
  • enter student registrations - from September 2020
  • enter student results - from 2021

You will be signed out if you are inactive for 30 minutes.

Before you start

You need a DfE Sign-in account and an assigned service role to use this service.

Service roles

T Levels Reviewer – reviews T Level titles and confirms or queries the details

Providers Editor – enters and amends T Level providers

Registrations Editor – uploads, amends, views, deletes and adds student registrations and assessment entries

Site Administrator – has all roles: T Levels Reviewer, Providers Editor, Registrations Editor
