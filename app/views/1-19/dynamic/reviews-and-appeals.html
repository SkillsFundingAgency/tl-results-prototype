Reviews and appeals

You must tell us if a component of a learner’s T Level is under review or being appealed for learners who have:

  • appealed a teacher-assessed grade for 2021
  • requested a review
  • appealed a review decision

While a component is under review or being appealed we cannot:

  • calculate the learner’s overall T Level result
  • issue a certificate
