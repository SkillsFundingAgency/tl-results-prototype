Reviews and appeals

If a learner has:

  • requested a review of marking, moderation or clerical check; or
  • appealed a review decision

you must use this service to confirm which component is under review or appeal by putting it 'on hold'.

While a component is on hold:

  • the learner's overall T Level result cannot be calculated
  • a certificate cannot be issued

Once the review or appeal is complete, you must confirm the learner's result and take the component 'off hold'. The learner's overall T Level result can then be calculated and a certificate can be issued.
