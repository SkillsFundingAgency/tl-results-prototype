Reviews and appeals

If a learner has:

  • requested a review of marking and moderation (RoMM); or
  • appealed a review decision

you must use this service to mark their certification as 'on hold'. This prevents an inaccurate certificate being printed while the review or appeal is in progress.

Once the review or appeal is complete, you must mark their certification as 'off hold' so the certificate can be printed.
